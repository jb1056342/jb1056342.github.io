What is Stunited?

Stunited is the first and only app that establishes a national community for students seeking to be matched with other students. Simply install and start networking; connecting with other students or tutors as needed. Free to install and use, Stunited members enjoy being able to barter their services or goods in exchange for tutoring on any number of subjects. Stop the struggle now and avoid paying costly tutoring fees to strangers who don’t understand your needs. Get Stunited!

STEP 1: Create Your Profile

Let people know just who you are and what you have to offer. If you’re a rockstar in any given academic subject or class, highlight that. If you’re simply in need of tutoring, editing or paper reviews, state what you’re willing to take on for the benefit of their services.

STEP 2: Find Other Students

With a simple search, click or swipe, students at all levels can start developing a network of academic assistance in just minutes. Search through a card stack of profiles for students that appeal to you, then instantly start messaging once a profile is selected.

STEP 3: Get Started Tutoring!

That’s it. It’s that simple. Select a student profile, discuss details of a problem or need via private chat, then connect. With Stunited, you’ll be able to get the assistance you need while using your gifts and abilities in order to help foster the growth of fellow students just like you. Get started now and stand Stunited!
