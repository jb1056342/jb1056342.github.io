About the App

Designed to help college students achieve higher levels of academic success, the Stunited educational networking app is both a connection-building tool and bartering system. Some may compare it to popular dating apps, but we know that Stunited goes far beyond a simple hookup. We help establish mutually-beneficial relationships that build the academic abilities and skills of students all over the world. It’s like bartering for knowledge.

How does it work?

First and foremost, it is imperative that all Stunited app users understand that we do not condone, or provide access to, paper-buying or plagiarizing. We adhere to the highest standards of academia and support all institutions in their efforts to ensure academic integrity.

That being said, and hopefully completely respected, the Stunited app works as simply as this:

John has never been great at math. It’s the one area that simply stumps him and he’s now afraid that if he doesn’t pass the next Geometry test with a fairly decent grade, he’ll fail the course. John’s not about to let that happen, but he also has no money to pay a tutor. What John does have is athletic skills and a passion for rock climbing.
So, after hearing the buzz about Stunited, John creates his own profile, selecting the areas of strength that he inhabits while letting others know that he could use some help in math. John waits.

Jane’s more than your average student. Successful in both humanities and science-based courses, Jane has a natural talent for math and has always been drawn to its theories and methods. Lately, college life has not offered all of the social opportunities that Jane was hoping for when she moved across state lines to go to school. Not a party-goer, she has been feeling like she would love to learn a new hobby or sport to round out her weekends a little more fully. Intrigued by classroom talk about the new Stunited app, Jane installs it and creates her own profile. She quickly sees John’s request for math tutoring. Thinking that learning how to rock climb may be just what she’s been hoping for, she reaches out.

John and Jane set up the agreement, the meeting times and just what the deal is between them. John passed his class with the help of Jane and Jane not only learned the basics of rock climbing but met some new like-minded friends that get her out of her dorm room, making her whole college experience that much richer.

How Do I get Started?

Simply install the app and follow the prompts. With just a few swipes and clicks, you’ll quickly establish a network of connections with other students on your same campus and across the nation. It’s a no-brainer way to get the assistance you need while avoiding paying expensive tutors. Show off your skills and use your own unique talents and abilities to get the services you need with our free, easy-to-use mobile app and stand Stunited!
